# Shipping Information

## Standard Shipping
- Delivery time: 3-5 business days
- Cost: Free for orders over $50
- Cost: $5.99 for orders under $50
- Tracking: Available online

## Express Shipping
- Delivery time: 1-2 business days
- Cost: $14.99
- Tracking: Available online
- Cut-off time: 2 PM EST

## International Shipping
- Delivery time: 7-14 business days
- Cost: Calculated at checkout
- Duties and taxes: Customer responsible
- Tracking: Available online

## Shipping Address
- We currently ship to all 50 US states
- APO/FPO addresses: Yes
- PO boxes: Yes
- International: Over 50 countries

## Order Tracking
- Visit our website and enter your order number
- Use the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email
- Contact support for assistance
