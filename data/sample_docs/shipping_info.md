# Shipping Information

## Shipping Options

We offer several shipping options to meet your needs:

### Standard Shipping
- **Delivery Time**: 5-7 business days
- **Cost**: Free on orders over $50
- **Cost**: $5.99 for orders under $50

### Expedited Shipping
- **Delivery Time**: 2-3 business days
- **Cost**: $12.99

### Overnight Shipping
- **Delivery Time**: Next business day
- **Cost**: $24.99

### International Shipping
- **Delivery Time**: 7-14 business days
- **Cost**: Varies by destination
- **Customs**: Duties and taxes not included

## Order Processing

- **Processing Time**: 1-2 business days
- **Cut-off Time**: Orders placed after 2 PM EST ship the next business day
- **Weekends**: Orders placed on weekends ship on Monday

## Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you'll receive:

1. An email with tracking information
2. A link to track your package online
3. Estimated delivery date

## Shipping Address

- We currently ship to all 50 US states
- APO/FPO addresses are supported
- Po Box addresses are accepted

## Signature Requirements

- Orders over $200 require signature upon delivery
- You can request signature confirmation for any order
- Delivery confirmation is included on all shipments

## Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package is lost or arrives damaged:

1. Contact us within 48 hours
2. Provide your order number
3. We'll investigate and either replace or refund

## Free Shipping Threshold

Free standard shipping applies to:

- Orders over $50 within the contiguous US
- Some exclusions may apply (large items, international)
- Sale items may have different shipping rates

## Shipping Carriers

We use multiple carriers to ensure reliable delivery:

- **USPS**: For standard and international shipments
- **UPS**: For expedited and overnight services
- **FedEx**: For large or heavy items

## Address Correction

If your address is incorrect or incomplete:

- We may contact you to confirm the correct address
- Additional fees may apply for address changes
- We're not responsible for packages delivered to incorrect addresses provided by the customer
