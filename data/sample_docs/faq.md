## Orders & Payments

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept:
- All major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
- PayPal
- Apple Pay
- Google Pay
- Shop Pay

### Can I change or cancel my order?
You can modify or cancel your order within 24 hours of placement. After that, the order may have already shipped.

### Do you offer gift cards?
Yes! Gift cards are available in denominations of $25, $50, $100, and $200. They never expire.

### Can I use multiple discount codes?
Only one discount code can be applied per order. The exception is stackable promotions, which will be clearly marked.

## Returns & Exchanges

### How do I return an item?
See our detailed Returns Policy. In summary:
- Items must be returned within 30 days
- Items must be unused and in original packaging
- Return labels can be printed online

### Can I exchange an item?
Yes! You can exchange items for a different size, color, or style. The same 30-day window applies.

### What if I receive the wrong item?
Contact us immediately. We'll send the correct item and provide a prepaid return label for the incorrect item.

## Shipping

### How long does shipping take?
Shipping times vary by method:
- Standard: 5-7 business days
- Expedited: 2-3 business days
- Overnight: Next business day

### Do you ship internationally?
Yes! We ship to over 100 countries. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination.

### Can I track my order?
Yes! You'll receive an email with tracking information once your order ships.

## Products & Warranty

### Are your products warranted?
Yes! All products come with a manufacturer's warranty. Warranty terms vary by product and manufacturer.

### Do you offer product repairs?
Yes! We offer repair services for electronics and appliances. Contact us for a repair quote.

### Can I get product recommendations?
Absolutely! Our product experts are available via chat or phone to help you find the right product.

## Account & Security

### How do I create an account?
Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner. You'll need:
- Email address
- Password
- Basic shipping information

### How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. We'll send you a link to reset your password.

### Is my personal information secure?
Yes! We use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal information. We never share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

## Loyalty Program

### How does the loyalty program work?
Earn points for every purchase. Points can be redeemed for discounts on future orders.

### How do I check my points?
Log in to your account and go to "My Rewards" to see your current points balance.

### Do loyalty points expire?
Points expire after 12 months of inactivity. Make a purchase to keep your account active.
