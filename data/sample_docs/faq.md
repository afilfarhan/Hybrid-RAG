# Frequently Asked Questions

## Order Status
**Q: How do I check my order status?**
A: You can check your order status by logging into your account and viewing your order history, or by using the order tracking link in your shipping confirmation email.

**Q: Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?**
A: You can change your shipping address within 24 hours of placing your order by contacting our support team.

## Payment
**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**
A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your payment information. We never store your full credit card number on our servers.

## Returns
**Q: How do I return an item?**
A: To return an item, contact our support team within 30 days of purchase to request a return authorization. We will provide you with a return label and instructions.

**Q: How long does it take to process a refund?**
A: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your returned item.

## Products
**Q: Do your products come with a warranty?**
A: Yes, all our products come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship.

**Q: Are your products compatible with international power standards?**
A: Most of our electronic products are compatible with international power standards (100-240V). Please check the product specifications for details.
