# Returns Policy

## Overview

Our returns policy is designed to be simple and fair. We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase.

## Eligibility

Items can be returned within 30 days of purchase for a full refund. Items must be:

- Unused and in original packaging
- With all tags attached
- In the same condition you received them

## Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

- Perishable goods (food, flowers, plants)
- Personal care items (makeup, skincare)
- Custom or personalized products
- Digital downloads

## How to Return

### Online Orders

1. Log in to your account
2. Go to "My Orders"
3. Select the order you want to return
4. Click "Return Items"
5. Follow the instructions to print a return label

### In-Store Returns

Bring your receipt and the item to any of our store locations. Our staff will process your return immediately.

## Refunds

- **Credit Card**: 5-7 business days
- **PayPal**: 3-5 business days
- **Store Credit**: Instant

Refunds include the original purchase price plus tax. Shipping costs are refunded only for defective items.

## Exchanges

If you'd like to exchange an item for a different size or color:

1. Return the original item
2. Place a new order for the desired item
3. We'll ship it immediately

## Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:

1. Contact us within 48 hours
2. Provide photos of the damage
3. We'll send a replacement or issue a full refund
